Aroma Bloom

Our dream is to light up your world with our handcrafted candles.

Founded in 2010 by Emma Green in upstate New York, our journey began with a passion for creating warmth and ambiance through handcrafted candles. What started in a small kitchen quickly gained a loyal following due to our dedication to quality and exquisite fragrances.

By 2015, we expanded from a home-based operation to our first workshop, allowing us to increase production while maintaining our artisanal standards. Collaborating with local artists, we designed unique candle holders to enhance our offerings.

Today, our meticulously handcrafted candles continue to inspire and delight, embodying our commitment to quality, sustainability, and beauty. Thank you for being a part of our story and helping us light up the world.
